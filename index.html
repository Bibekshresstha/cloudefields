About Cloudfields

Born from Curiosity.

Cloudfields is a playground for frozen imagination. We dream up ice creams inspired by places, textures, memories, and moments—then churn them into rich, indulgent realities. Every scoop is crafted to surprise, delight, and linger long after the last bite.

Our Belief

Crafted for Delight

At Cloudfields, we believe ice cream should feel like discovery. We explore flavors the way artists explore ideas: with curiosity, restraint, and just enough boldness to keep things interesting. From global inspirations to playful twists, every Cloudfields creation is made to feel indulgent, comforting, and a little magical.
